Recreational Soccer League

What you need to know!

This recreational soccer league is fun for all in the age range of 5 -12. Once sign-ups
have taken place, teams will be formed based on smaller age groups

Age Groups

  • 5 - 6
  • 7 - 8
  • 9 - 10
  • 11 - 12

Once split up into smaller age brackets, teams will be formed with the following choice
of names and team colors.

Team Names/Colors

  • Tigers - Orange and Black
  • Bears - Purple and White
  • Lions - Black and Gold

any reason you need more information, please feel free to contact me with the information below.
